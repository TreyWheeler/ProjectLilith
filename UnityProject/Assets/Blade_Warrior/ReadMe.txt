* Attack explanation

-Attack 00

 This motion is wield a blade Quickly once.

-Attack

 This is ranged attack.
 This motion is collected energe at Blade,and
  Attack to enemy far away.
  
-Attack01

 This motion is  defend from enemy 's attack, and Counter attack.
 
-combo

 This motion is attack three time in a row
 
 -Skil
 
  This motion is wield a blade on fire and strikes down on the ground.
  Then, Fire explosion occurs on around.
  
  -----------------------------------------------------
  
  If you make Run and attack motion,
  
  You chould be mixed animation [Run00]and [DrawBlade]
  or  mixed [Run]and [Attack00]
